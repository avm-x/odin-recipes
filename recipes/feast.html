Feast of the Warriors

Name: "Might of Mimir"

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs. of boar or wild pig meat, cubed
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • 4 carrots, chopped
  • 3 stalks celery, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bottle of mead (or strong ale)
  • 1 quart of beef broth
  • 1 sprig of thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

In a large pot, brown the meat in batches over medium heat. Remove meat from pot and set aside. Add onions, carrots, celery, and garlic to pot and cook until softened. Return meat to pot. Pour in mead (or ale) and beef broth. Add thyme and bay leaf. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat and cook for 2-3 hours, or until meat is very tender. Remove bay leaf and serve hot with crusty bread.
