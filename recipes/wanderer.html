The Wanderer's Snack

Name: "Loki's Lure"

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dried apples (thinly sliced)
  • 1/2 cup toasted almonds
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

Combine apples, almonds, honey, cinnamon, and salt in a large bowl. Toss to coat evenly. Spread mixture in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Dehydrate in a food dehydrator at 135°F (57°C) for 6-8 hours, or until completely dry and chewy. Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place.
